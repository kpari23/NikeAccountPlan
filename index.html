Business Risks & Pain Points
⚠️
High Risk
Fragmented create–plan–make–deliver–sell chain
Elliott Hill said it publicly: "we're moving at different speeds." No unified portfolio visibility across 3 brands, 5 sports, 4 geographies. Resource reallocation decisions happen without real-time data. This is the operational problem Planview is built to solve.
🔴
High Risk — Active RFP
Release orchestration platform replacement
Nike issued an RFP March 10 to replace XL Release (digital.ai). 31 functional requirements. 3,000 named users. September 1 go-live tied to holiday peak. Planview R&V submitted March 30. Awaiting shortlist decision.
📉
Medium Risk
China recovery slower than expected
Revenue down 16% in Q2. Digital down 36%. Nike has become "a lifestyle brand competing on price in China." Leadership extended the reset timeline. CFO needs real-time scenario modeling to model recovery investment options.
🌐
Medium Risk
Tariff headwind threatening margin recovery path
$1.5B annualized incremental product costs from U.S. tariffs. 320bps gross margin headwind (reduced to ~120bps net). Double-digit EBIT margins require surgical reallocation and real-time portfolio trade-off capability.
🏗
Medium Risk
Innovation pipeline must scale from Running to all sports
Running's 20%+ growth must be replicated across Football, Basketball, Training, and Sportswear simultaneously. Nearly 1,000 designers merged into one innovation org. No unified stage-gate governance process exists for the new structure.
